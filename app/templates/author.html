About Me

My name is Nick, and I'm the creator of Math.StackExpert. This site is my project for Insight Data Science, a six-week fellowship program in Palo Alto, CA that trains academics for careers as data scientists in the tech industry.

Before coming to Insight, I earned my PhD in Mathematics (and, briefly, was a postdoctoral lecturer) in the Mathematics department at The Ohio State University; my specialty was random graphs, the study of probability distributions on spaces of discrete structures. I am originally from Bloomington, MN.
